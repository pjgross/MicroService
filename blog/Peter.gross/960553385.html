Peter gross : 7) Infrastructure automation

Created by Peter Gross, last modified on Sep 15, 2020


One of the requirements of Microservices is that you automate as much of the development lifecycle as possible.

Important aspects to look at automating:

  • Automated testing, Testing lots of small components can be too time consuming if not automated


  • Automated deployments where in the past we were deploying larger components, we are now deploying lots of small components



For Microservices automation is essential:

  • Short deployment cycles rely on it
  • It can not be done manually
  • There are alot of tools to accomplish this



Motivation

  • Short deployment cycles
