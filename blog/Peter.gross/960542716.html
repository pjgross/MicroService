Peter gross : Why Typescript?

Created by Peter Gross, last modified on Sep 15, 2020


So why did I use Typescript for the backend development?


The microservices talk to each other over events, and will most probably be developed by different teams.

One of the key parts to microservices is to have a published api that is easily communicated to all teams that are using them.


Typescript allows us to define these Publishing and Listening events, and make their definitions available to the developers, and enforce them.



There are more benefits of using typescript, but it was used here to demonstrate that you need to implement some form of common interface definitions in your microservice architecture.
