Peter gross : Nats Streaming Service

Created by Peter Gross, last modified on Sep 12, 2020

The NATS Streaming service is a very efficient but simple Streaming service.

The key features that I wish were part of the service:

  • The ability to use delayed events so i did not have to actually implement the expiration service
  • Handle publishing retries if they fail (this is a missing feature in phase 1, and what a service mesh would do automatically)
  • Provide rate limiting on calls to the message service (another service mesh feature)

In Phase 3 of the project I will access my technology choices and one area I am considering is to use Kafka over Istio to get the best of both
